## Figure 1 — Sovereign Lattice: Component Topology

**File:** `figures/sovereign-lattice.svg`

**Purpose:** Shows the spatial arrangement of three components (c₁, c₂,
c₃) in a triangle, their current phases (sovereign, active, awakening),
their energy levels, and the symmetric bonds connecting them.  Safety
invariant badges confirm that ZERO_BLEED, PHASE_CANCELLATION, and
NOISE_FLOOR all hold in this configuration.

**Key elements:**
- Purple node: sovereign phase (c₁, energy = 2)
- Green node: active phase (c₂, energy = 1)
- Gold node: awakening phase (c₃, energy = 1)
- Blue lines: symmetric lattice bonds
- Gold energy indicators beneath each node
- Arabic invocation at top: بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

**Use in paper:** Primary topology diagram.  Appears in Section 2
(System Model) or as Figure 1.

## Figure 2 — Sentinel Layer: Guardian State Machine

**File:** `figures/sentinel-layer.svg`

**Purpose:** Depicts the Sentinel's three observation states (clear,
watching, alert) and the transitions between them.  Emphasizes that the
Sentinel is a pure observer — it never modifies phase, energy, or bonds.

**Key elements:**
- Green double circle: `clear` state (lattice fully dormant)
- Gold double circle: `watching` state (activity detected)
- Red double circle: `alert` state (bond integrity violated)
- Gray arrows: transitions with enabling conditions
- Self-loop on `watching`: SentinelScan with no state change

**Use in paper:** Sentinel architecture diagram.  Appears in Section 4
(Sentinel Layer) or as Figure 2.

---

## Figure 3 — Phase Lifecycle: dormant → sovereign

**File:** `figures/phase-flow.svg`

**Purpose:** Illustrates the four-phase lifecycle of a single
component, showing the forward transitions (Awaken, Activate, Ascend)
with their guards, and the backward Rest transition that returns any
non-dormant component to dormant.

**Key elements:**
- Four rounded boxes: dormant (gray), awakening (gold), active (green),
  sovereign (purple)
- Forward arrows with action names and guard conditions
- Dashed arcs for Rest(c)
- Safety invariant bar at bottom
- Arabic invocation with transliteration at top

**Use in paper:** Phase lifecycle diagram.  Appears in Section 3
(Phase Transitions) or as Figure 3.

## Combined Reference Sheet

**File:** `figures/one-page-diagrams.pdf`

**Purpose:** A single-page PDF containing all three diagrams arranged
vertically, suitable for printing, slide inclusion, or quick reference.
The sheet includes the Arabic invocation, all three panels, and
invariant badges.

---

## Regeneration

All figures are generated by `scripts/gen_figures.py`.  To regenerate:

```bash
pip install matplotlib
python3 scripts/gen_figures.py
```

Output is written to `figures/`.  The script is deterministic — running
it again produces identical SVGs and PDFs (modulo matplotlib version
differences in SVG metadata timestamps).

## Accessibility Notes

- Every SVG is pure vector and scales without loss.
- Every figure has a companion `.caption.txt` and `.alt.txt`.
- PDF variants embed fonts so Arabic renders correctly without system
  fonts.
- Dark background provides high contrast for all node colours.
- See `ACCESSIBILITY.md` for the full checklist.
